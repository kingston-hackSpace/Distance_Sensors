# Distance Sensors

At hackSpace, we have 2 types of distance sensors:

---
## [HC-SR04 Ultrasonic sensor](https://github.com/kingston-hackSpace/Distance_Sensors/blob/main/Ultrasonic.md)

Use an ultrasonic sensor when:

- You want a simple and reliable way to detect obstacles

- Lighting conditions may change

- Object color or surface shouldn’t affect the reading

---

## [Pololu IR Distance Sensor]

Use a Pololu IR distance sensor when:

- You need fast readings at short distances

- Space is limited and you need a compact sensor

- You are working indoors with controlled lighting
