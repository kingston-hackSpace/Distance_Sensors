# Pololu IR Distance Sensor - 300cm MAX

### DESCRIPTION 

This small lidar-based distance sensor reports the distance of objects up to about 300cm away with a pulsed (PWM) signal. A digital microcontroller pin can be used to time the length of each high pulse, which encodes the measured distance. 

### POWERING

The sensor works over an input voltage range of 3.0 V to 5.5 V.

### MESUAREMENT NOTES

The ***maximum range*** of 300 cm is only achievable for high-reflectance objects in good ambient conditions. Lower-reflectivity targets or poor ambient conditions will reduce the maximum range.

Please note that while this sensor can detect objects to within about 1 mm of the sensor face, the effective **minimum distance** it can measure is around 4 cm, so objects closer than 4 cm might result in an inaccurate measurement.
